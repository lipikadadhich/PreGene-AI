# Research Papers — Orientation

This folder is intended to hold locally-saved, real peer-reviewed
research papers (or their abstracts/summaries) relevant to the genes,
diseases, and CRISPR strategies PreGene-AI covers.

## Why this folder is currently empty

To keep the knowledge base trustworthy, this starter content
deliberately does **not** invent placeholder citations, fake study
names, or fabricated findings — doing so would risk the chatbot later
presenting made-up research as if it were real, which is exactly the
kind of hallucination a RAG system is meant to prevent.

## What to add here

- PDFs or text exports of real papers (e.g. from PubMed, journal sites
  you have rights to save/use, or your own institution's library access)
- A short text file per paper noting: title, authors, journal, year, and
  a plain-language summary of the key finding, if you want a lighter
  alternative to storing the full PDF

Once real papers are added, the citation engine will surface them with
the actual filename/title as the source, so answers grounded in this
folder stay traceable to genuine literature.
