# WHO Reference Resources — Orientation

The World Health Organization (WHO) publishes global classification
standards and public health guidance relevant to genetic conditions:

- **ICD (International Classification of Diseases)**: the global
  standard for classifying and coding diseases, including many genetic
  and congenital conditions, used for consistent reporting and health
  statistics across countries.
- **WHO guidance on genomics in public health**: periodic reports and
  guidance on the responsible use of genetic/genomic technologies in
  population health, newborn screening programs, and equitable access
  to genetic services globally.
- **Congenital disorders fact sheets**: WHO-published, publicly
  accessible summaries on the global burden of birth defects and
  congenital genetic conditions, aimed at informing public health
  policy.

## How this knowledge base should use them

This folder is intended to hold locally-saved WHO reference material
(fact sheets, guidance documents, classification excerpts) relevant to
PreGene-AI's supported conditions. As with the NIH references folder,
keeping the original WHO source noted in the filename or a document
header supports accurate citation.
